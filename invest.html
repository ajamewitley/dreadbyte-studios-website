Investor Overview

This section provides a concise, professional view of who we are building for, what we are building, and how capital would be used to scale our portfolio and studio capabilities.

For qualified, long-term partners

Vision & Mission

Dreadbyte Studios is an independent electronic studio focused on building dark, competitive experiences with a strong emphasis on replayability, clarity and tension.

Our mission is to create a compact portfolio of games that feel sharp from the first session, and deep enough to sustain long-term mastery—across dark fantasy, survival and competitive arena subgenres.

Market Positioning

We operate in the intersection of:

  • Dark fantasy and survival audiences seeking atmosphere and challenge.
  • Competitive players looking for high signal-to-noise gameplay.
  • PC-first ecosystems where discoverability and depth still matter.
Current Project Overview

Flagship — FZ Arena

FZ Arena is our competitive cornerstone: a fast-paced local multiplayer shooter with advanced realism and shooting mechanics. The project is currently in pre-alpha, with systems design and core feel under active development.

  • Platform: Android and iOS with a focus on responsive touch controls.
  • Target audience: players who enjoy high-stakes, skill-driven combat and realistic shooting mechanics.
  • Monetisation: fair cosmetics and expansions, no pay-to-win.

Existing Demos

We have shipped public demos for The Last Brethren and Zanni, establishing tone, world-building and early player expectations.

  • Both titles validate our core pillars: tension, atmosphere and clarity.
  • They act as R&D environments for systems, controls and visual language.
Funding Needs

Use of Funds

  • Core development: engineering, design, art, animation, UI and sound.
  • Production: tools, infrastructure, QA, build and deployment pipelines.
  • Marketing: trailers, key art, targeted campaigns and community building.
  • Online operations: servers and services required for FZ Arena and beyond.

Stage & Expectations

We are in an early phase, focused on product quality, stability and clear value propositions. Metrics, retention data and revenue figures will be surfaced as they become meaningful.

At this stage, we prioritise conversations with partners who understand the risk profile of early-stage game development and value disciplined scope and execution.
