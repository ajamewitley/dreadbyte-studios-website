Support is about belief, not equity. Every contribution helps us move faster: building content, sharpening systems and taking our dark competitive projects from demo to full release.

Ongoing Support

Patreon

Ideal for players who want to back the studio month to month and stay close to development.

  • Behind-the-scenes notes and early look at features.
  • Supporter role in community spaces.
  • Priority access for future playtests where applicable.
One-time support

Ko-fi / Buy Me a Coffee

For one-off boosts that help us cover tools, assets and production costs without any ongoing commitment.

  • Perfect for saying “keep going”.
  • Helps fund small but critical improvements.
Direct

Early Access Packs

Future early access offerings for FZ Arena and other titles will channel support directly into content and live-ops.

  • Access to early builds when available.
  • In-game cosmetics / titles for early backers.
