About Dreadbyte Studios

Dreadbyte Studios is an independent electronic studio focused on building dark fantasy and survival experiences with competitive edges—games that feel good to touch and demand respect from players.

Remote-first · Competitive-focused

Studio Story

We started Dreadbyte Studios with a simple idea: modern players deserve games that combine atmosphere and competitive clarity. The worlds can be brutal, but the rules must always be understandable and fair.

From early prototypes like The Last Brethren and Zanni to the upcoming fast-paced local multiplayer shooter FZ Arena, every project is a deliberate step toward tighter systems, better feedback and stronger identity.

Core Values

  • Clarity over chaos: tension comes from good decisions, not confusion.
  • Respect for players: no pay-to-win, no disrespectful monetisation.
  • Craft over noise: a small portfolio done well beats a crowded slate.
  • Competitive mindset: responsiveness, readability and fairness first.

Where We Operate

Dreadbyte Studios operates as a remote-first team with collaborators across regions. We embrace asynchronous workflows and production discipline to keep ambitions aligned with execution.

Future Goals

  • Solidify FZ Arena core gameplay and alpha.
  • Expand and refine existing demos based on player feedback.
  • Establish consistent pipelines for art, sound, live-ops and competitive balance.
  • Build a healthy community of players, collaborators and supporters who believe in our approach.
